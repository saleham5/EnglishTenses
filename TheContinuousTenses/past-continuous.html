Past Continuous الماضي المستمر

✔️ Form:

  • sub + was/were + v1 + ing

🧲 Usge:

  • حدث كان مستمر في فترة معينة في الماضي
  • يكون هنالك حدث مستمر ثم ياتي حدث اخر و يقطعه (كله بالماضي)

    • ❓Question & ⛔Negative

  • was | were
  • wasn't | weren't

    • ✨ Keywords:

  • Keywords: while | when

    • ❕Note

  • past continuous + when + past simple
  • past simple + while + past continuous

    • 😎 Examples:

  • I was watching TV when she called.
  • i was studying when she called (قطعه)
  • When the phone rang, she was writing a letter.
  • While we were having the picnic, it started to rain.
