Past Perfect الماضي التام

✔️ Form:

  • Sub + had + v3

🧲 Usge:

  • عند التحدث عن حدثين حصلوا ورا بعض
  • عند حدوث شي قبل/بعد شيء

    • ❓Question & ⛔Negative

  • had
  • hadn't

    • ✨ Keywords:

  • Keywords: after | because | by the time | before | as soon as

    • ❕Note:

  • ماضي تام past perfect الحدث الاول يكون
  • ماضي بسيط simple past الحدث الثاني يكون
  • simple past + after + past perfect
  • past perfect + before| by the time + simple past

    • 😎 Examples:

  • after i had studied, i watched TV
  • i had brushed my teeth before i went to bed
  • after i had eaten, i drank a cup of tea
